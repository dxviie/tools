Hershey Fonts
=============

Files: HersheySans1.svg, HersheyScript1.svg, HersheySerifMed.svg, HersheySerifMedItalic.svg

SVG versions prepared by Windell H. Oskay, www.evilmadscientist.com
Source: https://gitlab.com/oskay/svg-fonts

The Hershey Fonts were originally created by Dr. A. V. Hershey while working
at the U.S. National Bureau of Standards.

The format of the font data in this distribution was originally created by:
  James Hurt
  Cognition, Inc.
  900 Technology Park Drive
  Billerica, MA 01821

USE RESTRICTION:
  This distribution of the Hershey Fonts may be used by anyone for any
  purpose, commercial or otherwise, providing that:

  1. The above acknowledgements are distributed with the font data.

  2. The font data in this distribution may be converted into any other
     format EXCEPT the format distributed by the U.S. NTIS (where each
     point is described in eight bytes as "xxx yyy:").
